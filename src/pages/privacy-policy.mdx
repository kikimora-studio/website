            # Privacy Policy

            **Last updated: March 2026**

            This Privacy Policy describes how Kikimora Studio ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use our website (kikimora.studio) and the Kikimora Studio mobile application ("the App").

            ---

            ## What Data We Collect

            The App uses Google Firebase Authentication to manage sign-in. When you authenticate via Facebook or Google, Firebase receives and stores:

            - Your public profile name
            - Your email address associated with that account
            - A unique user identifier (UID) generated by Firebase

            This is the only personal data we collect. We do **not** collect health data, location data, payment information, device identifiers, or usage analytics.

            ---

            ## How We Use Your Data

            Your authentication data is used solely to:

            - Create and manage your session bookings
            - Display your name on your booking confirmation
            - Identify you when you sign back in

            We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or advertising.

            ---

            ## Data Storage and Security

            Your authentication data is stored and managed by Google Firebase, hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Firebase applies industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest. For details, see the [Firebase Privacy and Security documentation](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

            We retain your data only for as long as you have an active account. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

            ---

            ## Third-Party Services

            The App relies on the following third-party services for authentication:

            - **Google Firebase Authentication** — [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
            - **Facebook Login** — [Meta Privacy Policy](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/)
            - **Google Sign-In** — [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

            We do not share your data with any other third parties.

            ---

            ## Your Rights

            You have the right to:

            - Request access to the data we hold about you
            - Request deletion of your data and Firebase account
            - Withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account

            ---

            ## Contact

            If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

            **Email:** [info@kikimora.studio](mailto:info@kikimora.studio)
